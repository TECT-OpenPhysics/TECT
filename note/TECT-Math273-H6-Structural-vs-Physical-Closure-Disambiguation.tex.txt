\begin{enumerate}[label=(\roman*)]
\item Algebraically self-contained (no missing parameters).
\item Parametrically complete on textbook or separately-proved bases.
\item Domain-independent (does not change functional form when underlying geometry changes, per base-manifold-independence analysis).

The T6 tier applies to the **formal derivation** of the expression. It does not imply numerical accuracy or phenomenological truth.

\textbf{PHYSICAL T4 STRONG EVIDENCE} means:

The **numerical value** of quantity $X$, when substituted with actual microscopic parameters and validated against experimental observables, agrees with known measurements within specified precision. This requires:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\roman*)]
\item Numerical parameter extraction (e.g., $a_{\rm BCC}$ from condensation data).
