**How to navigate this site.** The website mirrors the canonical
repository structure:

- **Theory** — axiom A0, Brazovskii regime, locked parameters, 11-pillar Stage-1 scorecard, and the Math60 Stage-1/2/3 rubric.
- **Notes** — full Math01–Math84 list of theorem notes, auto-generated from `Docs/math/TECT-Math<NN>*.tex.txt` headers.
- **Code** — live `Codes/` modules, solver versions, audit tools.
- **Results** — dated $R$-tags, manifests, retraction notices, key numerical anchors.
- **History** — chronological CHANGELOG mirror.
- **Records** — Active OPEN-QUESTIONS + NEGATIVE-RESULTS ledger.
- **Papers** — Papers I–IV source locations and status.

To answer a question about a specific claim, follow the seven-question contract documented in `Docs/status/INDEX.md`.
