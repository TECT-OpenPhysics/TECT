## 12. Failure-mode handling

If any phase encounters an unforeseen failure mode not covered in
the relevant Math note's failure-mode taxonomy, the response is:
1. Honest scope acknowledgment in the affected Math note's
   AUDIT-FLAGGED status.
2. Open a follow-up Q-task with explicit failure-mode classification.
3. Defer the affected Phase closure until the failure mode is
   resolved or explicitly carved out (per Math302 carve-out
   precedent).

The 20-turn arc deadline (estimated 2026-08-01) allows budget for
3-5 failure-mode handling cycles without disrupting the overall plan.
