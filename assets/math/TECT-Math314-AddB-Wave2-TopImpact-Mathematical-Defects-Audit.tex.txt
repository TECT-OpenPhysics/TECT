\section{Trigger and audit context}
% ===========================================================================

The parent Math314 (Aux-01/02 + Epoch-01/02) and Math314-AddA
(Epoch 03--12) audits established the wording-calibration discipline
for Wave-6 and Wave-7 papers. The hostile-referee audit on 2026-05-02
extended to the four Wave-2 Top-impact papers (Paper-TI-1 through
Paper-TI-4) and uncovered defects of \emph{higher} severity than the
Aux/Epoch wording over-claims:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.4em]
\item \textbf{Paper-TI-1}: actual mathematical inconsistency in the
main theorem (rank-dependence elided; rank-1 line-bundle context
conflated with rank-16 fermion-sector specialisation; arithmetic error
in Step~3 of the proof sketch).
\item \textbf{Paper-TI-2}: structural over-claim presenting a
single-mode-approximation (SMA) / equal-amplitude / first-shell
truncation ranking as a ``rigorous unique global minimum theorem''.
\item \textbf{Paper-TI-3}: \emph{fatal logical defect}: the original
proof Step 2 invokes ``time-reversibility of the classical dynamics''
to support a unique-microscopic-Cauchy-datum claim, but the underlying
TDGL flow is dissipative and NOT time-reversible; additionally,
$\langle \Psi \rangle = 0$ is conflated with a unique microscopic
boundary state, and ``axiom count reduction $3 \to 2$'' is
mis-counted.
\item \textbf{Paper-TI-4}: standard mathematics is correct; the
TECT-application phrasing was over-claim relative to the current
canonical Pillar-4 realisation programme on
$\Sigma_0 = \mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$ (Math305) and the
Math302 proof-rigor / realisation-gate carve-out.
\end{itemize}

The present addendum documents these corrections, repairs the
mathematics where required (TI-1, TI-3), recasts theorem statements
where required (TI-2), and downgrades scope where required (TI-4).

\subsection*{Paper-TI-1 corrections (mathematical repair)}

The original main theorem stated $\mathrm{ind}(D_E^c) = 16 - \mu$ as a
general theorem for a holomorphic vector bundle $E$ on $\mathbb{CP}^2$
with $c_1(E) = 0$ and $c_2(E) = \mu H^2$. The actual integration in
the proof yields, by the spin-c Hirzebruch--Riemann--Roch formula on
complex 2-fold $\mathbb{CP}^2$ with canonical spin-c structure
($c_1(L_{\rm can}) = 3H$):
\[
\mathrm{ind}(D_E^c) \;=\; \int_{\mathbb{CP}^2}
\mathrm{td}(T\mathbb{CP}^2) \wedge \mathrm{ch}(E)
\;=\; r - \mu,
\]
where $r = \mathrm{rk}(E)$. The original $16 - \mu$ wording is the
specialisation $r = 16$ (relevant to the SO(10) chiral-fermion sector
of the TECT Pillar-4 programme), NOT a general theorem.

\textbf{Restructured corrected file (Paper-TI-1, in this commit)}:
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.6em]
\item \textbf{Main theorem}: $\mathrm{ind}(D_E^c) = r - \mu$ for
general rank $r$, $c_1(E) = 0$, $c_2(E) = \mu H^2$ on $\mathbb{CP}^2$
with the canonical spin-c structure. Proof gives the explicit
degree-graded integration:
\[
[\mathrm{td} \wedge \mathrm{ch}]_{(4)} = H^2 \cdot r + \tfrac{3}{2} H \cdot 0 + 1 \cdot (-\mu H^2) = (r - \mu)\, H^2,
\]
which integrates to $r - \mu$ via $\int_{\mathbb{CP}^2} H^2 = 1$.
\item \textbf{Corollary} (rank-$16$ specialisation): $r = 16
\Rightarrow \mathrm{ind} = 16 - \mu$ for the SO(10)-fermion sector.
\item \textbf{Separate remark} on the Picard classification
$\mathrm{Pic}(\mathbb{CP}^2) \cong \mathbb{Z}$, explicitly NOT mixed
with the rank-$r$ index formula. The rank-$1$ Picard context (with
$c_2 = 0$) is geometrically distinct from the abstract rank-$1$ index
calculation that would formally give $1 - \mu$ for $c_2 = \mu H^2$.
\end{enumerate}

The arithmetic error in the original Step~3 (mixing degree-$0$ and
degree-$4$ contributions in the integration) is removed by the
explicit degree-graded computation above.

\subsection*{Paper-TI-3 corrections (fatal logical defect repair)}

The original Theorem~1 ``A2 Axiom Reducibility'' claimed
\[
\text{A2} \Leftrightarrow (\text{A0} + \text{A1}) \quad
\text{at} \quad t = -\infty \quad \text{under cosmological } T(t).
\]
This claim is supported in the proof by Step 2 (``backward
extrapolation by time-reversibility'') and Step 4 (``unique
causally-determined boundary condition''). Both steps fail for the
following independent reasons:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.6em]
\item The dissipative TDGL flow $\partial_t \Psi = -\Gamma\, \delta
\mathcal{F} / \delta \Psi^*$ is NOT time-reversible. The ``backward
extrapolation by time-reversibility'' invocation is structurally
incorrect; backward-uniqueness of the microscopic Cauchy datum cannot
be obtained from forward dissipative TDGL.
\item $\langle \Psi \rangle = 0$ is an ensemble / phase-class
characterisation of the symmetric high-temperature regime; it is NOT
a unique microscopic Cauchy datum at $t = -\infty$. Many distinct
microscopic configurations are compatible with the same phase-class
behaviour.
\item The ``effective axiom count reduces from 3 to $2_{\rm core} +
1_{\rm cosmological}$'' framing mis-counts: the total premise count
is preserved at 3; the actual change is reclassification (2 internal
axioms + 1 external boundary input).
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Corrected wording (Paper-TI-3, in this commit)}:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.6em]
\item Title: ``On the reclassification of A2 in TECT: from an
independent ultra-high-energy postulate to a high-temperature
symmetric-phase boundary class'' (NOT ``reducibility'').
\item Abstract: explicit ``weakening / reclassification, NOT
elimination''; explicit two-part structural caveat (TDGL irreversibility
$+$ ensemble-vs-microscopic distinction); explicit total-premise-count
preservation.
\item Theorem~1 (relabelled ``A2 weakening / reclassification''):
states that A2 ``need not be imposed as an independent microscopic
dynamical law'', and ``can be replaced by the weaker requirement that
the early-time state belongs to the symmetric high-temperature phase
class $\langle \Psi \rangle = 0$''. Total premise count preserved
$3 = 2_{\rm internal} + 1_{\rm boundary}$.
\item New Remark explaining why this is weakening, not elimination
(TDGL irreversibility + ensemble-microscopic gap).
\item Step 2 of the proof sketch rewritten to remove the
``time-reversibility'' invocation and replace it with forward
consistency.
\item Step 4 of the proof sketch rewritten to use a phase-class
argument rather than a unique-Cauchy-datum argument.
\end{itemize}

\section{Quantitative sanity check (CLAUDE.md §6.3.4)}
% ===========================================================================

\textbf{TI-1 mathematical defect: arithmetic verification.} For
$r = 16$, $\mu = 0$ (canonical Math179--198 realisation), the corrected
formula gives $\mathrm{ind}(D_E^c) = r - \mu = 16$. For $\mu = -40$
(originally claimed Scenario A), the corrected formula gives
$\mathrm{ind}(D_E^c) = 16 - (-40) = 56$, matching the original Scenario
A as a special case. The corrected general formula therefore reproduces
both the originally claimed $16$ and the originally rejected $56$ as
two specialisations of the same rank-$r$ formula at $\mu \in \{0,
-40\}$. The relation
\[
56 - 16 \;=\; 40 \;=\; -\mu \quad \text{(Scenario A)}
\]
provides a non-trivial cross-check on the arithmetic.

\textbf{TI-3 quantitative-sanity on the ``axiom count'' claim.} Original
$N_{\rm axiom}^{\rm TECT} = 2_{\rm core} + 1_{\rm cosmological} = 3$.
Corrected: total premise count = 3 (preserved). The corrected
``reduction factor'' in the original arithmetic
$\rho_{\rm reduction} = N^{\rm before} / N^{\rm after} = 3 / 2 = 1.5$
is structurally vacuous (the denominator $2$ counts internal axioms
only and excludes the boundary input; the numerator $3$ counts all
premises). The honest reclassification statement preserves the total
count and reports only the structural shift.

\textbf{TI-4 canonical-base sanity.} The current canonical Pillar-4
base $\Sigma_0 = \mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$ has Hodge numbers
distinct from $\mathbb{CP}^2$:
\[
h^{1,1}(\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1) = 2 \neq h^{1,1}(\mathbb{CP}^2) = 1,
\quad
h^{2,0} = 0 \text{ for both.}
\]
The Picard rank therefore differs ($\mathrm{Pic}(\mathbb{P}^1 \times
\mathbb{P}^1) \cong \mathbb{Z}^2$, NOT $\mathbb{Z}$), which is one
concrete reason the $\mathbb{CP}^2$ result of TI-4 cannot be
transferred verbatim to the current canonical Pillar-4 base. A
separate $\Sigma_0$ companion note is required for the canonical
realisation chain; this is not derived in the present audit.
