\section{Canonical Locked-Basis Reading of the Longitudinal Actual PDE Coefficient}

\begin{equation}
\boxed{

=
\frac12\Tr\!\bigl(\Sigma_3\,\hat n^j\mathcal U_j(0)\bigr).
}
\label{eq:CLK_upar_trace}
\end{equation}

In the diagonal Pauli basis \(\Sigma_3=\mathrm{diag}(1,-1)\),
\begin{equation}
\boxed{

=
\frac12
\Bigl[
(\hat n^j\mathcal U_j(0))_{11}

\label{eq:CLK_upar_diag}
\end{equation}

So \(u_\parallel\) is literally the shell-normal diagonal asymmetry of the reduced \(J\)-block.
