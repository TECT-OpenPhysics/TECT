§ 2. THE THREE LEMMA CHAIN
=============================================================

L1 (Math202).  PROVED unconditional.
---------------------------------

   On the smooth manifold CP^2 equipped with the standard Fubini-Study
   complex structure,

       H^1(CP^2, O) = H^2(CP^2, O) = 0   (Hodge / Dolbeault)

   and consequently

       Pic(CP^2)  ≅  H^2(CP^2, Z)  ≅  Z

   via the first Chern class. Every U(1)_χ bundle on the Math162 base
   is uniquely determined (up to bundle isomorphism) by an integer
   k_χ = c_1(U(1)_χ) ∈ Z. Note: Pic^0(CP^2) = 0 (a corollary of (A)),
   so the smooth and holomorphic classifications coincide.

L2 (Math203).  PROVED CONDITIONAL on RR1.
-----------------------------------------

   On (CP^2, σ_I) with σ_I antiholomorphic, σ_I^* h = −h on H^2(CP^2,Z)
   (Atiyah 1966). For any σ_I-equivariant U(1)_χ bundle L, equivariance
   σ_I^* L ≅ L + naturality of c_1 give

       c_1(L) = σ_I^*(c_1(L)) = − c_1(L)   in H^2(CP^2, Z) ≅ Z

   forcing c_1(L) = 0.

   The σ_I-equivariance is automatic for any bundle constructed from
   the Math162 atlas (whose transition functions are I-equivariant by
   construction; Math162 §2). The identification of σ_I as
   antiholomorphic (rather than holomorphic) on the Math162 CP^2 base
   is RR1: it follows from the chirality-flip 16 ↔ 16-bar of parity on
   the SO(10) spinor (Peskin-Schroeder Eq. 3.131) restricted to the
   SU(5)-orbit projectivization (Math80-AddA §3.4). The bookkeeping
   of this restriction is RR1.

L3 (Math204).  PROVED CONDITIONAL on RR2.
-----------------------------------------

   π_2(SO(10)/SU(5)) = π_3(SO(10)/SU(5)) = 0 (Slansky 1981 + standard
   homotopy LES). The Pontrjagin-Chern relation

       c_2(E_{SU(5)})  =  p_1(E_{SO(10)}) / 2

   is equivalent to the SU(5) cocycle being homotopically T-valued.
