\section{Pairwise compatibility matrix $C_{ij}$}

\begin{definition}[Compatibility relation $C_{ij}$]
For $(i, j) \in \{1, \ldots, 11\}^2$, define:
\begin{itemize}
\item $C_{ij} = +1$ (mutual support): $H_i$ and $H_j$ refer to the
       same locked $\Psi^*$ and there exists a configuration $\Psi^*$
       satisfying both simultaneously, with $H_i$ providing input or
       compatible structure for $H_j$ (or vice versa).
\item $C_{ij} = 0$ (no constraint / orthogonal): $H_i$ and $H_j$ refer
       to distinct sectors with no direct interaction; both can hold
       independently.
\item $C_{ij} = -1$ (incompatible / conflict): there exists no $\Psi^*$
       satisfying both $H_i$ and $H_j$ simultaneously; the pair is
       inconsistent.
\end{itemize}
By definition $C_{ij} = C_{ji}$ (symmetric); $C_{ii}$ is undefined.
\end{definition}
