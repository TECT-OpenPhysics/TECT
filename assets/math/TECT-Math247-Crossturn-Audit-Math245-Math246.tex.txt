\begin{quote}
Every cross-turn audit that reviews a note citing another TECT Math note must include a **"Cited-Canonical-Fact Spot-Check"** subsection. For each cited numerical result (e.g., "$c_2(E) = 1$", "$\mathrm{ind} = 56$", "$\mu = -40$"), the auditor must:
