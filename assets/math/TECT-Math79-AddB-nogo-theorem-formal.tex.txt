$$[\hbar] = [M] \cdot [M]^{-2} \cdot [M]^{-1} = [M]^{-2}.$$

\begin{align}

$$[\hbar] = [E][T] = [M] \cdot [T] = [M] \cdot [M]^{-1} = [M]^0.$$

So $\hbar$ is **dimensionless** in natural units!
