\subsection*{2. Spin-2 decomposition: Traceless and trace modes}

The strain tensor is:
\begin{equation}
e_{ij} = \partial_i u_j + \partial_j u_i.
\label{eq:strain-tensor}
\end{equation}

Decompose into trace and traceless parts:
\begin{equation}
e_{ij} = e_{ij}^T + \tfrac{1}{3}\delta_{ij} e_k^k,
\end{equation}
where $e_{ij}^T = e_{ij} - \tfrac{1}{3}\delta_{ij} e_k^k$ satisfies $\text{tr}(e^T) = 0$.

The elastic energy is:
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{H}_{\rm el} = \tfrac{\lambda}{2}(e_i^i)^2 + 2\mu\,(e_{ij}^T)^2
= \tfrac{\lambda}{2}(\partial_k u_k)^2 + 2\mu\,(e_{ij} - \tfrac{1}{3}\delta_{ij}\partial_k u_k)^2.
\end{equation}

Expand $(e_{ij} - \tfrac{1}{3}\delta_{ij} e_k^k)^2$:
\begin{align}
(e_{ij}^T)^2 &= e_{ij}^2 - \tfrac{2}{3}e_{ij}\delta_{ij}(\partial_k u_k) + \tfrac{1}{9}\delta_{ij}\delta^{kl}(\partial_k u_k)(\partial_l u_l) \\
&= e_{ij}^2 - \tfrac{2}{3}e_k^k \cdot e_k^k + \tfrac{1}{9}(e_k^k)^2 \\
&= e_{ij}^2 - \tfrac{5}{9}(e_k^k)^2.
\end{align}

Therefore:
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{H}_{\rm el} = \tfrac{\lambda}{2}(\partial_k u_k)^2 + 2\mu\,\left[e_{ij}^2 - \tfrac{5}{9}(\partial_k u_k)^2\right]
= 2\mu\,e_{ij}^2 + \left(\lambda - \tfrac{10\mu}{9}\right)(\partial_k u_k)^2.
\end{equation}

For stability, we require $\mu > 0$ and $\lambda + 2\mu/3 > 0$ (so bulk modulus is positive).
At the operating point, $\lambda = -0.43 < 0$ but $\lambda + 2\mu/3 \approx -0.43 + 2(1.62)/3 \approx 0.64 > 0$. ✓

\subsection*{9. Resolution: Careful handling of coefficient conventions}

The issue arises from the definition of $h_{ij}^{TT}$. In standard GR,
the metric perturbation is $h_{\mu\nu}$, and the convention is:
\begin{equation}
h_{ij}^{TT} \equiv \text{perturbation to } g_{ij} = \delta_{ij} + h_{ij}.
\end{equation}

The strain in elasticity is:
\begin{equation}
u_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}(\partial_i u_j + \partial_j u_i).
\end{equation}

The identification is $h_{ij}^{TT} = 2u_{ij}^{TT}$ (factor of 2 from GR convention).

With this convention:
\begin{equation}
h_{ij}^{TT} h_{ij}^{TT} = 4 u_{ij} u_{ij}.
\end{equation}

The elastic energy is:
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{H}_{\rm el} = 2\mu(u_{ij}^T u_{ij}^T) = \frac{\mu}{2}(h_{ij}^{TT} h_{ij}^{TT}).
\end{equation}

Thus:
\begin{equation}

= \frac{1}{64\pi G}(\partial_i h_{jk}^{TT})^2.
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
\mathcal{L}_{\rm el}^{\rm kin} = \frac{\rho_{\rm cond}}{2} \times \frac{1}{4}(\partial_t h_{ij}^{TT})^2 = \frac{\rho_{\rm cond}}{8}(\partial_t h_{ij}^{TT})^2

This gives $\rho_{\rm cond} = \frac{1}{8\pi G}$.
