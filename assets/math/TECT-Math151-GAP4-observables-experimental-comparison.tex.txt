\subsection*{Part I: CMB Scalar Spectral Index}

\subsubsection*{Theoretical framework (Math147)}

From Math147 (Kibble-Zurek + linear-response theory):

The primordial power spectrum for scalar perturbations:
$$P_\zeta(k) = \frac{H^2}{2\pi c_s^2} |u_k|^2 \bigg|_{k=aH},$$
where:
\begin{itemize}
\item $H$ = Hubble parameter at horizon exit.
\item $c_s$ = effective sound speed (BCC phonon dispersion at relevant scales).
\item $u_k$ = scalar mode amplitude (from Brazovskii power-spectrum prediction).
\end{itemize}

The spectral index:
$$n_s = 1 + \frac{d \ln P_\zeta}{d \ln k}.$$

**TECT prediction** (from Brazovskii critical exponents, Math146):
$$n_s^{\rm TECT} = 1 - \epsilon_s,$$
where $\epsilon_s = 2/23 \approx 0.087$ (Brazovskii universality class, $\nu = 2/3, z = 7/3$).

Thus:
$$n_s^{\rm TECT} \approx 0.913.$$

\subsubsection*{Falsification criterion F1}

\textbf{Expected vs. observed}:
\begin{itemize}
\item TECT prediction: $n_s^{\rm TECT} \approx 0.913$.
\item Planck 2018: $n_s^{\rm exp} = 0.9649 \pm 0.0042$ (68\% CL).
\item Discrepancy: $\Delta n_s = 0.913 - 0.965 = -0.052$ (5.2σ tension).
\end{itemize}

**Status F1: FAIL** (unless correction factors from nonlinear analysis or higher-loop RGE adjust $\epsilon_s$).

**Mitigation pathway**:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Compute 1-loop RGE correction to critical exponents (Math75-Q2, Pillar 4 research item).

\item Defect density: $n_{\rm defect} \sim \tau_Q^{-0.78}$ (freeze-out).
\item Defect annihilation lifetime: $\tau_{\rm ann} \sim \xi / v_s$ (sound crossing time).
\item GW energy density: $\Omega_{\rm GW}(f) \propto (\xi / \ell_{\rm Hubble})^2$ at frequency $f$ (characteristic defect-oscillation scale).

$$\Omega_{\rm GW}(f) \sim f^{1/2} \quad \text{(incompletely damped regime)}.$$

**Status F2: PASS** (TECT predicts GW background well below current constraints, consistent with observations).
