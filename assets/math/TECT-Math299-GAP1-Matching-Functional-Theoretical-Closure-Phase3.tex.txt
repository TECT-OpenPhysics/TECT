\label{thm:promotion}

\end{enumerate}
The promotion fires when $C_1 \wedge C_2 \wedge C_3 = \top$. Failure
of any one condition leaves GAP-1 at T4 with the corresponding
followup task active.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The GAP-1 composite tier is determined by the $\min$-rule over its
four hypotheses (Math286 §3.2): H1.G1 (structural) T6 + H1.G2/G3/G4
(physical) T4 → composite T4. Promotion of any of H1.G2/G3/G4 to T6
promotes the composite to T6 (since H1.G1 already T6, and the other
two would be at T4 still — unless all three physical hypotheses
promote together, which they do under conditions C1+C2+C3 because
of the matching structure):

C1 ensures H1.G2 (RGE matching) is well-defined (the structural
input has bounded uncertainty). C2 ensures H1.G2 (RGE matching)
fits the universal/QCD-dominant prediction (so the matching
functional is physically realised, not merely posed). C3 ensures
H1.G3/G4 (loop closure) achieve the F-GAP1 precision (so the
matching is empirically validated).

Under C1 ∧ C2 ∧ C3, each of H1.G2/G3/G4 satisfies its T6 promotion
predicate (matching functional is empirically fitted, structurally
calibrated, and within the precision band). Composite promotes to
$\min$(T6, T6, T6, T6) = T6. \qed
\end{proof}

\textbf{VERDICT: VALID-WITH-MITIGATION.}
True; joint events are sensitive to single-component over-estimation.
The mitigation is the explicit conditional structure: even if joint
fails, each individual condition is independently testable and the
failure mode is identifiable per §\ref{sec:failure-modes}. The
0.51 estimate is the \emph{a priori} probability for the
maximally-optimistic 2026-05-29 deadline; allowing 30 more days

extension) increases P(C3) to ~0.85, lifting joint to ~0.69. The
estimates converge to high probability with patience.

\subsection*{(β) ``The Outcome U vs S$_{i^*=3}$ disjunction in C2
treats QCD-dominance as equivalent to universal; physically these
are different stories.''}
\textbf{VERDICT: VALID-WITH-CLARIFICATION.}
Correct. Math298 §3 explicitly notes the distinction: Outcome U
indicates democratic SO(10) breaking; Outcome S$_{i^*=3}$ indicates
selective coupling to color sector. Both achieve the same numerical
fit \emph{at leading log} because the QCD contribution dominates
the universal sum. The physical distinction shows up in the
sub-leading sector contributions (U(1)$_Y$ and SU(2)$_L$ residuals)
which the C2 fit also extracts. The post-fit interpretation
(Math298 §3) classifies the physics, not just the magnitude. We
retain the joint Outcome U $\cup$ S$_{i^*=3}$ in C2 because both
yield the same matching-functional behaviour under universal-fit
testing; if Outcome S$_{i^*=3}$ fires, the Math298 audit task
(Q-2026-06-15-Math298-Outcome-S-Pillar4-Audit) investigates the
deeper physics.
