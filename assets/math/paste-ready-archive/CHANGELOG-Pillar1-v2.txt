LIMITATIONS & CAVEATS
---------------------

1. Single-mode approximation (SMA):
   - Assumes all condensate modes lie on single shell |k_i| = q_0
   - Multi-shell corrections are O(r/(κq_0^4)) ~ 10^-3, acceptable for global ordering
   - Does not address quasicrystalline or incommensurate phases

2. Equal-amplitude ansatz:
   - Protected by Michel's theorem (spherical t-design optimality)
   - Amplitude-relaxation analysis (Math03) confirms local minimum
   - Higher-order corrections ~ sqrt(r) suppress amplitude deviations

3. 1-loop accuracy:
   - Higher loops (2-loop, 3-loop) contribute corrections ~ u² << 1
   - Dominant N_loop hierarchy is stable against higher orders
   - No phase-structure instability expected above 1-loop

4. Single-scalar order parameter:
   - Does not address multi-component condensates (spin, flavor, color)
   - Real TECT may require extended internal-symmetry analysis
   - Pillar 1 scope covers scalar sector; multi-component remains open
