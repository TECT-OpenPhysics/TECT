\subsection*{6. Numerical Result Gate (CLAUDE.md §6.3.3 Mandatory)}

Every numerical claim in the 10-turn block (e.g., $m^{*2}_{\text{num}}, R_{32}^{\text{TECT}}, \Delta \eta^{(2)}, \Lambda_{\text{obs}}$) must carry the (cause, evidence, falsification criterion) triple before being treated as evidence in higher-tier notes:

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Cause}: which numerical solver / pipeline produced this value (Math82-H, Math36-extractor, etc.).
\item \textbf{Evidence}: convergence criterion ($\|F\|$, $\lambda_{\min}$, RMS residual), interval-arithmetic bound (if applicable), and multi-scale / multi-seed validation.
\item \textbf{Falsification criterion}: pre-registered test whose failure requires the value to be demoted to OUTLINE or retracted.
\end{itemize}

Example: Math82-H $m^{*2}_{\text{num}}$ must record (cause: ground-state continuation curve; evidence: $\|F\| < 10^{-8}$, $\lambda_{\min} > 10^{-4}$ at all 7 $\mu^2$ points; criterion: ratio $m^{*2}_{\text{num}}/m^{*2}_{\text{analytic}} \in [0.8, 1.2]$ for promotion to PROVED).
