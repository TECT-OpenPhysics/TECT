DECISION 2 (Projection onto first-shell):

Argument: Full functional is infinite-dimensional, intractable analytically. First shell
(12 modes) captures the critical long-wavelength physics. Higher shells are massive,

(Kadanoff-Wilson blocking theorem).

Justification: Reduces problem to manageable size; captures essential physics; error

g^(c) (cubic-harmonic anisotropy). Higher-dimension operators suppressed by power counting.
Decision: Retain 3 couplings {λ₀, λ_g, g^(c)}.
Justification: Minimal truncation that captures the discrete-to-continuous mechanism.
Subleading couplings corrected at higher loop order.

DECISION 4 (Regulator choice):
