### §6.2 Objection β: Composite Stage-2 T3 is misleading

**Statement**: Four gates with composite tier T3 (driven by GAP-4 T3) looks worse than three gates at T6. This obscures the fact that GAP-2 and GAP-3 are **robust at T6** while GAP-4 is the **unique weak point**. Reporting "Stage-2 = T3" is **misleading**.

**Counter-argument**:
